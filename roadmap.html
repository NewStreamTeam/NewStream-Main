Upcoming Features

Here's what we're planning for the next major releases. These features are in the design or early development phase and are subject to change.

  • Integrated chat system: An integrated Discord-like channel system to easily link creators and their community.
  • Creator Monetization Tools: We don't generate money from this project, so we can't monetize creators. BUT, we are planning to integrate a donation button for creators.
  • Integrated sponsor-to-creator communication tool: To make creator's life easier.

In Progress

These features are currently being actively developed by our team and contributors. We're working hard to bring them to you soon!

  • Video upload system: Manage server-side video upload
  • UI/UX: Make the UI as clean and easy to use as possible
  • Optimization: Fix bugs and improve overall performance

Completed Features

A look back at what we've recently delivered. Thank you to all our contributors for making these possible!

  • Supabase Database structure
  • Primitive video upload system: Stored in a supabase bucket (yes)
  • Categories: Possibility to add tags on your videos

Ideas & Suggestions

We value your input! If you have an idea for a new feature or an improvement, please share it with us.

You can submit your ideas through our Discord or GitHub issues page.
